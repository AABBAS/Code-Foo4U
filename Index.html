How many ping pong balls can a school bus hold?

1) Google search and vet the top results to find the volume of the common US American school bus is: 2511ft^3.

2) Use the same google search method to find the volume of the ping pong ball used in the USA table tennis league is 2.045 in^3.

3) Convert both measurements into like terms. Divide the size of the common ping pong ball (2.045 in^3) by 12 so as to make it like terms with the volume of the school bus. The new ping pong size is .17ft^3.

4) Divide the volume of the school bus by the newly converted volume of the ping pong ball. (2511ft^3/.17ft^3).

5) Rounding to the nearest ones place, the common US American school bus can hold 14770 ping pong balls.
